About Me

My name is Muhammad Humam Arij, a student in the Information Systems program at the Open University of Indonesia with a cumulative GPA of 3.31. I completed my secondary education at SMK Bina Nusa Slawi with an average final grade of 84.34. With a background in graphic design, I have continued to develop my interest and skills in information technology, particularly in web development, programming, and digital information systems. I believe that the integration of visual creativity and technical skills is a valuable asset in creating effective, functional, and user-centric digital solutions. I actively participate in various projects, both individually and as part of teams, to refine my skills and broaden my professional horizons. With a commitment to continuous learning and a passion for making a positive impact, I am open to challenging opportunities in the IT field that support cross-disciplinary skill development.
