About Me
Introducing, I am Muhammad Humam Arij, a graphic designer with a great interest in the creative world and technology. I graduated from Bina Nusa Slawi Vocational School majoring in Multimedia, and currently continuing my studies at the Open University majoring in Information Systems.

With an educational background in multimedia and information technology, I am used to working on various graphic design projects, ranging from logo making, social media design, posters, to other visual needs. In addition, I also continue to develop my skills in the digital field, including understanding how design can be aligned with the needs of today's information systems and technology.

For me, design is not just about appearance, but how to create visual works that can convey messages effectively, attractively, and according to purpose. I always try to produce designs that are simple, strong, and easily understood by the audience.
